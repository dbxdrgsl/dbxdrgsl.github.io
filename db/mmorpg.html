It’s Retro O’Clock

This Unity project is a multiplayer open‑world role‑playing game with a nostalgic aesthetic.

  • Players explore a dynamic world filled with quests, puzzles and mini‑games.
  • Non‑player characters feature adaptive behaviours driven by finite‑state machines.
  • The game earned first place at FiiCode 2020, showcasing its technical and creative merit.

The design emphasizes player agency, modular architecture and replayability.
