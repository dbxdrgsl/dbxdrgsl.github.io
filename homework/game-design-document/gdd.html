Designing a Multiplayer Open‑World Role‑Playing Game

This game design document outlines the structure and mechanics of a large‑scale role‑playing game.

  • It defines the narrative arc, character progression and world topology.
  • Core mechanics include exploration, crafting and cooperative challenges.
  • Technical sections describe networking, asset pipelines and AI patterns.

The document informed the development of “It’s Retro O’Clock”.
