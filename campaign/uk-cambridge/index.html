Clear outcomes in 6–8 weeks

We target enquiry skills and language functions. Evidence is collected weekly.

Thinking & Working Scientifically

Plan, test, measure, explain. From hypotheses to conclusions with uncertainty awareness.

Data & Algorithms

Block diagrams, if/else, variables. Simple sensors or virtual labs to gather and reason with data.

Academic English

CLIL scaffolding: describe → explain → compare → conclude. CEFR‑aware prompts and sentence frames.

Built around Cambridge Lower Secondary

We use Thinking & Working Scientifically as the backbone, draw contexts from Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and finish with a Global Perspectives mini‑inquiry.

What this means in class

  • CLIL staging: input → guided output → free output.
  • Dual targets: science objective + language function.
  • Artefacts: lab notes, diagrams, short videos, viva.

Safeguarding & operations

  • KCSIE‑aware delivery; GDPR‑compliant data flow.
  • Hybrid or on‑site. 60–75 min sessions. Groups ≤ 16.
  • Resourcing: micro:bit/virtual labs; printable booklets.
