Numerical Approximation of PDEs using FreeFem++

In this thesis I studied the numerical solution of partial differential equations.

  • I used the FreeFem++ platform to discretise elliptic and parabolic PDEs via finite element methods.
  • Error analysis and convergence studies confirmed the accuracy of the approximations.
  • Applications included heat diffusion and Poisson problems on complex domains.

The work balances mathematical rigour with computational implementation.
